VeriGuard NFT bridges your digital assets with utility, offering seamless Discord verification with royalties and rewards management for NFT creators and collectors.

What We Offer

Our platform empowers you to verify NFT ownership for exclusive Discord roles and manage royalty distributions effortlessly. Whether you’re a collector securing community access or a creator ensuring fair compensation, VeriGuard has you covered. Currently the platform is tailored to erc-721 only, however erc-1155 compatibilitiy is in development.

NFT Verification

Link your wallet to our bot system via a secure verification process. Select your NFT and network (Songbird, Flare, or Basechain), and gain instant access to gated Discord communities based on your ownership—no repetitive logins required.

Royalties System

Creators can deploy royalty contracts to track and distribute earnings from NFT sales. Collectors can collect accrued balances and claim any time while unsold NFTs accrue their own shares of royalty claims adding incentive for buyers of any registered collection. Our system ensures transparency and fairness in the Web3 ecosystem.
